# Jamitex 🧵
### Pakistan's Textile Industry — Connected on One Platform

---

## What is Jamitex?

- A dedicated B2B platform built **exclusively for Pakistan's textile industry**
- Works like **Foodpanda for textile brokers** — brokers connect vendors to manufacturers digitally
- Brokers are the core users — the platform empowers them, not replaces them
- Any broker in Multan can now connect a vendor in Faisalabad to a manufacturer in Karachi — without a single phone call

---

## The Problem

- Pakistan's textile industry contributes **60%+ of total national exports** yet runs on phone calls and WhatsApp
- Brokers spend hours making calls just to find who has stock available right now
- Vendors in Multan are invisible to manufacturers in Karachi
- Manufacturers are limited to sourcing from people they personally know
- Deals that should take minutes take days
- Everything is tracked in notebooks, memory, and scattered WhatsApp messages

---

## The Solution

- One platform where brokers see **every vendor's live stock** and **every manufacturer's open requirements** simultaneously
- Full filtering by material type and city
- Broker connects the right vendor to the right manufacturer in minutes
- Every deal tracked digitally from start to completion
- What used to take 20 phone calls now takes 2 minutes

---

## How It Works — Three User Portals

### 🏭 Vendor Portal
- List available raw material stock — cotton, yarn, polyester, greige fabric, dyed fabric, thread, dyes, chemicals
- Specify quantity, unit (kg / meters / bales), grade, price per unit in PKR, and city
- See all active listings with live status — available, in progress, or sold

### 🏗️ Manufacturer Portal
- Post raw material requirements — material type, quantity, grade, deadline, and city
- See all active requirements with live status — open, in progress, or fulfilled

### 🤝 Broker Dashboard *(core of the platform)*
- See vendor stock listings and manufacturer requirements **side by side**
- Filter both sides by material type and city instantly
- Connect a vendor listing to a manufacturer requirement with one click
- Create and manage deal cards from initiation to completion
- Track full deals history — active and completed

> **Critical rule:** Vendors and manufacturers cannot see or contact each other directly. Every connection goes through the broker. The platform does not eliminate brokers — it makes them 10x more powerful.

💡 What Is Jamitex?

Jamitex is a dedicated B2B web platform built exclusively for Pakistan's textile industry. It gives textile brokers a single dashboard where they can see every vendor's live stock and every manufacturer's open requirements — simultaneously, filtered by material type and city — and close deals digitally without a single phone call.


🔴 It is not an ecommerce store
🔴 It is not a general B2B directory
🔴 It does not replace brokers
✅ It is a daily-use professional tool — the operating system for Pakistan's textile brokers
✅ It makes brokers 10x more powerful by giving them total market visibility in one place

🔒 The Core Design Principle


Vendors and manufacturers cannot see or contact each other directly at any point on the platform.



This is the most important design decision in Jamitex. The broker remains the essential human link in every transaction. Jamitex does not disrupt the broker — it gives the broker superpowers.

Before Jamitex — a broker handles 5 deals a month, limited by phone calls and personal contacts.
After Jamitex — the same broker can handle 50 deals a month, working with vendors and manufacturers across all of Pakistan from one screen.


⚙️ How It Works — Step by Step


Vendor logs in → fills a simple form → stock is listed on the platform instantly
Manufacturer logs in → fills a simple form → requirement is posted on the platform instantly
Broker logs in → sees both sides simultaneously on their dashboard
Broker filters by material type and city to find the right match
Broker clicks Connect → a deal card is created linking the vendor listing to the manufacturer requirement
Broker facilitates the deal between both parties using their contact details
Broker marks deal as In Progress → then Completed
Deal is logged in broker's history for full transparency and record keeping
